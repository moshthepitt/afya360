About Afya360

Afya360 is a modern and easy to use directory of health facilities in Kenya. Search and find hospitals, dispensaries, clinics and other health facilities anywhere in Kenya. Quickly access hospital name, information, phone numbers, addresses, directions and more.

Our mission is to help liberate data and information about health facilities in Kenya so that you, and others, can benefit from this.

The data that you find on this site is courtesy of the Ministry of Health of Kenya and the Kenya National Bureau of Statistics through the Master Health Facility List and the Kenya Open Data Initiative respectively.
